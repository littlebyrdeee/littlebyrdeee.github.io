Our Story: Brittney and Christopher shared two classes together their first semester at Baylor University. With an immediate connection, the pair attended Baylor's first football game of the year. That day they rode a mechanical bull, shared a snow cone and rushed the field after the Bears narrowly defeated their devilish rivals, the TCU Hornfrogs. To commemorate the special occasion, the couple has a brick in the new stadium that reads: "9/2/11 We Met. It was a win. B & C forever." Softly after, Christopher joined the Baylor Crew team to spend time with Brittney, which required arduous 4:30 a.m. workouts. He asked her to be his girlfriend by drawing a fairytale book ending with the statement "...and this is only the beginning of our story." The couple maintained their close friendship even while spending months apart as Brittney studied abroad in Europe. Christopher popped the question on Brittney's 22nd birthday in the pouring rain near the spot they first met on Baylor Campus, after reciting the 22 reasons he loved her. Brittney did not say yes. She cried and later mustered the words "of course."
