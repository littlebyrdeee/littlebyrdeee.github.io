Brittney & Christopher

Brittney & Christopher met Freshman year at Baylor University. Despite different majors, Chris and Britt had two classes together and had an immediate chemistry--like they had been lifelong friends! Chris invited Brittney to attend the first Baylor football game of the year, where they rode a mechanical bull, shared a snow cone and rushed the field after the Bears narrowly defeated their devilish rivals--the TCU Hornfrogs.

The couple has a brick in the new stadium that reads: "9/2/11 We Met. It was a win. B & C 4ever"

Chris joined the Baylor Crew team, although it required arduous 4:30 a.m. workouts, just to get to spend more time with Brittney. He asked Britt to be his girlfriend by creating a fairy-tale book which ended with the statement "...and this is only the beginning of our story." Britt & Chris maintained their best-friendship even while spending months apart when Britt studied abroad in Europe. The question was popped on Brittney's 22nd birthday in the pouring rain on Baylor's campus.
