Out-of-town guests will have various budget needs and trip desires, so we did not block rooms in one single hotel. However, we can provide some recommendations. We are staying at Hotel Triton, which is walking distance of the fireworks.
There are various hotels with free SFO airport shuttle and parking.
The closest hotel to the venue is the Laurel Inn. It is walking distance if you do not mind hills.
There are nice beach hotels south of the city, including Half Moon Bay and Santa Cruz.
If you are on a tight budget, consider renting a room in an Airbnb apartment, a hostel or a motel.
